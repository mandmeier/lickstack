A note from the creator


"What on earth do I play over those changes?"
"Why do I keep playing the same boring old stuff?"
"Why can't I remember that awesome solo I came up with last week?"

If you play jazz, blues, country, rock, pop or any other form of music that uses lead sheets, you may have asked yourself the same questions.

The idea behind the LickStack is to create a repository for short audio snippets of music to serve as a source of examples and inspiration for beginners and experts alike.

Join in, have fun and happy soloing!
- supreme_lickster
This website is currently under development:
I am neither a professional musician nor a professional web developer, but I am passionate about both and my goal is to make the LickStack as user friendly as possible. If you encounter any catastrophic errors or if you have any suggestions please contact me under info@lickstack.com.

Here's how it works


1. Find a chord sequence

This may be a passage in your lead sheet you are curious about. By design, licks on the LickStack are short patterns up to 4 measures long.



2. Search the LickStack

Find any licks that match your chord sequence using a simple search form. You can also specify your favorite instrument and keyword tags to find the exact genre and style you are looking for.

Browse Licks


3. Record and share your own licks!

Your licks will be displayed in an interactive panel. Play the recording as many times as you want, in any key! The examples below match the above changes:
