A note from the creator

Dear Licksters,

I keep asking myself:
"What on Earth do I play over those changes?"
"Why do I keep playing the same boring old stuff?"
"Why can't I remember that killer solo I came up with last week?"

If you play Jazz, Blues, Country, Rock, Pop or any other form of music that uses lead sheets, chances are you may have asked yourself the same questions.
The idea behind the LickStack is to create a repository for short snippets of music to serve as a source of examples and inspiration. This resource should be helpful to beginners and experts alike and bring musicians from all walks of life together.

Happy soloing!
- supreme_lickster

This web application is currently under development:
As I am neither a professional musician nor a professional web developer, my time to work on this project is limited. Nonetheless, I will do my best to keep improving the LickStack and make it as user friendly as possible as our collection grows! If you encounter any catastrophic errors please contact me under info@lickstack.com.

Here's how it works


1. Find a chord sequence

This may be a passage in your lead sheet that you are struggling with. By design, licks on the LickStack are short patterns up to 4 measures long.



2. Search the LickStack

Find any licks that match your chord sequence using a simple search form. You can also specify your favorite instrument and keyword tags to find the exact genre and style you are looking for.

Browse Licks


3. Listen and learn!

Licks will be displayed in an interactive panel. Play the recording as many times as you want, in any key! The examples below can be played over the above changes:
